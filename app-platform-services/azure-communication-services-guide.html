Get started

From zero to "hello world" in four steps

A consistent onboarding path whether you're adding SMS, email, calling or chat.

Create a resource

Provision an Azure Communication Services resource in the Azure portal or via ARM/Bicep.

Choose a channel

Select voice, video, chat, SMS, email or advanced messaging for your scenario.

Install the SDK

Add the .NET, JavaScript, Java, Python, iOS, Android or PowerShell SDK to your app.

Authenticate & send

Issue an identity token or use connection string, then send your first message or make your first call.

Communication channels

Meet your customers where they are

Click each channel to see capabilities, common scenarios, and the SDKs available.

📧 Email

Send transactional and bulk email at scale from your own verified domain — the recommended path for workloads throttled by Exchange Online TERRL / RRL limits.

  • REST API, SDKs or SMTP AUTH relay
  • Custom domain support with SPF/DKIM/ARC
  • Delivery analytics, bounce & suppression lists
  • EU Data Boundary supported
.NETJavaPythonJavaScriptSMTP
📧

📱 SMS

Send and receive SMS messages using toll-free numbers, short codes or long codes — for alerts, 2FA, reminders and two-way conversations.

  • Toll-free, short-code and alphanumeric sender IDs
  • Opt-out handling and delivery reports
  • Global coverage with region-specific compliance
  • Event Grid webhooks for inbound messages
.NETJavaPythonJavaScript
📱

☎️ Phone Calling (PSTN)

Purchase phone numbers and make/receive PSTN calls directly from your application or route calls through your existing SBC via Direct Routing.

  • Outbound and inbound calling from the cloud
  • Direct Routing via your SBC — bring your carrier
  • Emergency calling and dynamic location
  • Integrates with Call Automation for IVR & bots
.NETJavaScriptiOSAndroid

💬 Chat

Persistent, multi-party chat threads with typing indicators, read receipts, message history and file/image support.

  • Group threads with 250+ participants
  • Message history & edit/delete semantics
  • Event notifications for presence & typing
  • Pairs with Teams interop for hybrid threads
.NETJavaPythonJavaScriptiOSAndroid

Embed high-quality 1:1 and group audio/video calls into web, iOS and Android apps using the Calling SDK or the drop-in UI Library.

  • Join Teams meetings as a custom-branded user
  • Composable UI Library for React, iOS & Android

Order confirmation & shipping emails
